Home Decor

Manasvini

The complete hand-block printed range of fabrics named Manasvini”.
“Manasvini” is originated from Sanskrit and translates to english as “a proud woman”.

This label of hers features captivating hand-block printed rugs, bed sheets, cushion covers and curtains curated together just for you. Get creative and experiment with vibrant hues and block prints. Add a style statement to your home with an array of home accents featuring rare designs and an exclusive make.

“As the complete label is named after women we have categorized our prints with different Sanskrit names like Malu, Yosa, Yuvati, and so on. All of which mean “woman”.

Home is where the heart is” and thus we always wish to make it as comfy, as beautiful as distinct as possible. If you wish to do the same, take a look at the brand new handpicked collection of home-décor meant to take your breath away. Redefine your living space with Manasvini by I Design Spaces.
This Dehradun based Interior firm not only works on various interior projects but also works on promoting their own art and craft in a different manner. Hand-block printing has a rich history that can be traced back to the fifth century B.C., with its roots in India. As with most ancient practices, every process in hand- block printing, including the preparation of cloth, is labor intensive and time-consuming.
